About Us

CN Smokeless Company, LLC is a proud manufacturer of smokeless tobacco products. Our brands are made right here in the USA. As an innovative tobacco company, we offer both traditional moist smokeless tobacco, as well as snus. Our products are made to high quality standards at our modern facility in North Carolina, the heart of American tobacco country. As our business grows, we still maintain the foundation it was created upon: providing our adult tobacco consumers with a quality product at a fair price.

Products

Klondike

Our flagship brand, Klondike is available nationally in long cut, fine cut and pouches. All eight styles of Klondike Premium Moist Tobacco deliver freshness and superb taste to adult consumers at an everyday low price.

Decade

Available in eight long cut, fine cut and pouch varieties, Decade Premium Moist Tobacco competes in the discount price category. The brand offers excellent value for adult consumers who are looking for a quality product at a value price.

Derringer

Derringer provides adult consumers with high-quality smokeless tobacco at a discount price. Available in long cut, fine cut and pouches, it is a brand sold nationwide and competes in the price-value arena.

Nordic Ice

Our snus was developed using the authentic Swedish process. Nordic Ice Snus delivers excellent, quality tobacco in a spit-free pouch. It is sold nationwide and available in 3 styles: Frosted Mint, Original Wintergreen, and Wild Cherry.
